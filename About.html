ABOUT ME

Born and raised in Conselheiro Lafaiete, Brazil, I found my calling in technology early on. I honed my technical skills through rigorous academic training, earning a Technical degree in Information Technology from the Federal Institute of Minas Gerais, Ouro Branco Campus. Currently, I'm broadening my expertise at the Federal University of São João del-Rei, pursuing a degree in Computer Science.
My journey has been marked by diverse experiences, such as mentoring peers in programming during my technical studies and contributing to various projects. These projects span the development of software for computers, including an RPG game with an interface, an educational chat system akin to WhatsApp, and a mobile app developed with Kotlin, later enhanced using Flutter. Furthermore, my commitment to social improvement shone through the development of a school notes integration system aimed at enhancing public school administration.

I pride myself on my proficiency in a multitude of programming languages and technologies, including Java, JavaScript, Kotlin, Dart, PHP, Python, HTML5, CSS3, MySQL, MongoDB, and SQL Workbench. Additionally, my understanding of C/C++, C#, Pascal, and my capability in tools like Photoshop, Lightroom, Sony Vegas, and HitFilm Express underscore my versatility.With intermediate proficiency in English and basic knowledge of Spanish, I am poised to collaborate on international platforms and embrace global opportunities. My portfolio also highlights certifications from various courses and workshops, showcasing my relentless pursuit of excellence and learning.As I navigate through the complexities of programming and development, I am fueled by a vision to create impactful solutions that resonate on a global scale.
